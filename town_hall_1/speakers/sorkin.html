New York Times Columnist and author. Andrew Ross Sorkin has been described as "the most famous finnancial journial of his generation." His book, Too Big to Fail, was the first behind-the-scenes account of thqe financial crisis that led to our current recession.

The Economist, The Finnancial Times, and Bussiness Week all named Too Big To Fail one of the best books of the year. The book was published by Viking October 20th, 2009. The book was adapted as a movie by HBO Films and premired on HBO on May 23, 2011
